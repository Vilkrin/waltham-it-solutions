Our Story

Founded with a mission to bridge the technology gap for home users and small businesses, Waltham IT Solutions has grown from a local IT support service into a trusted networking specialist serving customers across London Borough of Waltham Forest and Surrounding areas.

We understand that technology should work for you, not against you. That's why we focus on delivering solutions that are not only technically excellent but also easy to understand and use.

Our specialisation in Ubiquiti UniFi systems allows us to provide enterprise-grade networking solutions at accessible prices, helping our customers enjoy reliable, secure, and fast connectivity.
